SkillSwap is a modern website that connects students with tutors for one-to-one learning.

The website helps visitors explore tutors by subject, compare profiles, and choose the right tutor based on goals, budget, and availability. It also includes account creation and secure login, where people can sign up as either students looking for lessons or tutors offering lessons. The overall experience is designed to make finding the right learning match simple, clear, and trustworthy.

Stack: Flask
